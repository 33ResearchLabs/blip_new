## 8. FAQ

**How long does a trade take?**
Most trades complete in 5–15 minutes. After you place an order, a merchant typically accepts within 1–2 minutes. Payment confirmation and escrow release happen in real time.

**Is my money safe?**
Yes. Every trade uses on-chain escrow — your USDC is locked in a smart contract until both sides confirm. If anything goes wrong, funds are returned to you.

**What payment methods are supported?**
Bank transfer (IBAN), UPI, and cash (in-person). You can save multiple payment methods and switch between them per trade.

**What happens if there's a problem with my trade?**
You can raise a dispute at any point after escrow is locked. Our compliance team reviews the chat history, payment proof, and blockchain records to resolve it fairly.

**Do I need a Solana wallet?**
Not necessarily. Blip includes a built-in embedded wallet that works automatically. If you prefer, you can connect an external wallet like Phantom.

**Are there any fees?**
A small platform fee (1.5–2.5%) is included in the rate, depending on the speed tier you choose. There are no hidden charges.
